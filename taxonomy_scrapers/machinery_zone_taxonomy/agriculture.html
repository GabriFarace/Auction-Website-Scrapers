Purchase and sale of used farm equipment

Present in more than 20 countries

15,000 new classified ads a day

More than 15 years' expertise

Founded in 2000 by three French farmers, the Agriaffaires site is a classified ad platform for the purchase and sale of used and new farming equipment. With more than 15,000 ads onlined every day, it includes farming, winegrowing and forestry equipment, as well as green space maintenance equipment. Our site also contains ads for rental and auction sales. Available in more than 20 languages, Agriaffaires offers farming equipment sellers and buyers a price Observatory to enable them to assess the average price trends for tractors, seed drills, micro tractors and sprayers, etc.. Thanks to over 15 years' expertise and content renewed every day, more than 4,500 professionals confidently use our site to buy or sell their used farming equipment. Agriaffaires has a twin platform, Machineryzone, dedicated to the sale of construction/public works, handling and transport equipment.
